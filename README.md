## What is this?

Anticonsumption is a 2-minute, 4-question quiz that helps you identify your natural creative style. In a world designed for scrolling, it's a small reminder that you are not just an audience.

You get one of six results: **Thinker, Maker, Storyteller, Connector, Builder, or Nurturer** — each with a description of how you create and what that means for you.

---

## Features

- 4-question quiz with instant, auto-advancing selections
- 6 distinct creator personas with tailored descriptions
- Share results directly to Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp
- Spark — an idle-triggered mascot that nudges you to make something
- Manifesto page explaining the philosophy behind the project
- Fully responsive
